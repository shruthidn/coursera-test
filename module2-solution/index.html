Our Ice Cream Menu

Venilla

Vanilla is frequently used to flavor ice cream, especially in North America and Europe.Vanilla ice cream, like other flavors of ice cream, was originally created by cooling a mixture made of cream, sugar, and vanilla above a container of ice and salt.

Mango

The "Ice Cream" cultivar was discovered in Trinidad and Tobago, and was later brought to the United States by Maurice Kong of the Rare Fruit Council International. It is of unknown parentage.It became recognized for its semi-dwarf growth habit.

Chocolate

Chocolate ice cream is ice cream with natural or artificial chocolate flavoring. Chocolate is the second most common flavor of ice cream in the United States, after vanilla.
